Electric Bike Maintenance

Electric bike maintenance is crucial for optimal performance and longevity. Regularly check and clean the battery connections, ensure the tires are properly inflated, and inspect the brake system. Lubricate the chain and other moving parts to prevent wear. Update firmware if applicable, and store the bike in a cool, dry place. Proper care enhances safety and extends your e-bike's lifespan.

Wheel service & repair
Wheel service and repair are essential for maintaining optimal bike performance and safety. This involves inspecting, truing, and balancing wheels, as well as replacing damaged spokes and hubs. Regular maintenance ensures smooth rides, prolongs wheel life, and prevents accidents. Brake and Tyre change
Brake and tire changes are crucial for bike safety and performance. Regularly inspecting and replacing worn brakes ensures effective stopping power, while new tires provide better grip and reduce the risk of punctures. Wheel Alignment
Wheel alignment is crucial for the safety and efficiency of your vehicle. Proper alignment ensures tyres wear evenly, improves handling, and enhances fuel efficiency. It involves adjusting the angles of the wheels to the manufacturer's specifications. Regular alignment checks can prevent uneven tyre wear, reduce strain on suspension components, and provide a smoother, more comfortable driving experience.

Brake Servicing

Brake servicing is crucial for vehicle safety, ensuring responsive and reliable stopping power. It involves inspecting, cleaning, and replacing brake pads, rotors, and fluid. Regular maintenance prevents wear, reduces noise, and improves performance. Professional servicing detects issues early, enhancing safety and prolonging the lifespan of braking components, making it essential for all vehicle owners.

General Servicing

Servicing is essential for maintaining the performance, safety, and longevity of machinery and vehicles. It includes routine inspections, cleaning, repairs, and part replacements. Regular servicing prevents breakdowns, ensures efficiency, and identifies potential issues early. For vehicles, this encompasses engine checks, oil changes, and brake inspections. Professional servicing keeps equipment in optimal condition, enhancing reliability and value.

Gear Servicing & Repairs

Gear servicing and repairs are vital for smooth and efficient machinery operation. This process involves cleaning, lubricating, and adjusting gears to ensure optimal performance. Regular maintenance prevents wear, reduces noise, and extends gear life. Professional repairs address issues like misalignment and tooth damage, ensuring reliability and precision. Proper gear servicing enhances overall equipment functionality and prevents costly downtime.
